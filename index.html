~Greetings & Welcome to Makehuman 2~

 The Project Makehuman 1 served a long and historic tour, far surpassing
its expecations!

 However the project has been unfortunately since all but abandoned, in
favor of plugins or other pay to use software due to its present limitations
and lack of support.

 The idea, however is still relevant and very good!

 This leads us to present day Makehuman 2, a brand new project of pure
passion and dedication. This current software was created from the ground
up by a very small but determined team. Intended for a new generation & an
existing community of 3d artists who choose to use stand alone-human creation
software!

We have again exceeded all expectations and shall continue to do so!

~Credits & Thanks to:

Punkduck (Head Software Dev & Coder) &
Elvaerwyn (Head 3d Artist, Dev/Coder & Creator)
